# Privacy Policy (The Narrator)

Last updated: 2026-03-06

This Privacy Policy explains what data The Narrator Discord bot (the "Bot") processes and why.

## 1. Data We Process

Depending on used features, the Bot may process and store:
- Discord IDs (user, guild, channel, message, role)
- Character and profile data you submit (names, bios, metadata)
- Economy data (points, wallets, upgrades, slot ownership)
- Server configuration (admin roles, logs channel, shop settings)
- Operational logs and interaction metadata
- Payment event metadata (if payment features are enabled)

The Bot does not require your Discord password.

## 2. Why Data Is Processed

Data is processed to:
- Provide bot commands and gameplay/RP features
- Persist server/user settings and economy progress
- Operate moderation/admin utilities configured by server admins
- Prevent abuse and troubleshoot service issues
- Verify and apply paid entitlements (if enabled)

## 3. Legal Basis (General)

Where applicable, processing is based on legitimate interest in operating and securing the Bot, and on user/server actions requesting bot functionality.

## 4. Data Sharing

Data is not sold.
Data may be shared only when necessary with:
- Hosting/infrastructure providers
- Discord monetization/payment partners and payout processors (for payment features)
- Legal authorities when required by law

## 5. Data Retention

Data is retained while needed for service operation or until deleted by admin/user action, subject to reasonable backup and legal retention needs.

## 6. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## 7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on jurisdiction and server controls, you may request:
- Access to stored data related to your user ID
- Correction or deletion where feasible
- Removal of bot data by removing the bot from a server

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under the minimum age required by Discord or local law.

## 9. International Processing

Data may be processed in countries different from your own, depending on hosting providers and infrastructure.

## 10. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: crazyland.support@gmail.com

## 11. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
